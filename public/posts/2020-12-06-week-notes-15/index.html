  • Deep Rock Galactic has been a lot of fun this week (it’s still on Xbox Games Pass). Initially I wasn’t particularly excited by the game, the first few missions were pretty repetitive and I didn’t like not being able to play same the mission again when we failed. The progression is very slow, but the upgrades to equipment when you do finally unlock them feel worthwhile.
